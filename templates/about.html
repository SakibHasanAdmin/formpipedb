Our Mission: Simplify Data for Everyone

We believe powerful tools should be simple, accessible, and designed to save you time.

The Story Behind FormPipeDB

FormPipeDB was born from a shared frustration among developers: the tedious, repetitive setup required for even the simplest projects. We found ourselves constantly wrestling with complex database configurations just to get a basic backend running.

We believe that dealing with data shouldn't be a chore. Our mission is to make powerful database tools accessible to everyone, not just seasoned experts. As a small team of developers passionate about efficiency, we decided to build the solution we wished we had: a tool that bridges the gap between a simple spreadsheet and a complex database, making SQL intuitive and visual.

Our Philosophy

Simplicity is Power

We believe that the best tools are the ones that get out of your way. Every feature in FormPipeDB is designed to be intuitive, requiring minimal setup and no steep learning curve.

You Own Your Data

Your data is yours, period. We provide the tools to manage it, but you always have full ownership and control. Export your entire database as a standard SQL file at any time, with no lock-in.

From Visuals to Code

Our platform is built to be a learning tool. By allowing you to build databases and queries with a visual interface, we help you understand the underlying SQL that makes it all work. It’s the perfect bridge from no-code to pro-code.
